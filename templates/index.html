Bark classification
    About
    barkclassification.ml is a web application for recognizing a tree species based on a photo of its bark. For this purpose, a deep learning TensorFlow model was used. Given database contains more than 23,000 photos of twenty species located in forests and parks near Quebec City, Canada. Pictures were taken at a distance of 20-60 cm from the trunk. For added variety, photos were taken with four different devices:
    1. LG Nexus 5
    2. Samsung Galaxy S5
    3. Samsung Galaxy S7
    4. Panasonic Lumix DMC-TS5
    To increase the variability, photos were taken under a number of weather conditions.
